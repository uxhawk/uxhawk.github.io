New Customer Onboarding User Research

Summary

Project Directive

Create an educational and engaging onboarding experience for new app users. The onboarding initiative needed to reduce the number of calls from new users and increase enrollment rates in notification and location services.

My Contributions

As the lead UX designer I on the project:

  1. Conducted user research and usability testing.
  2. Wrote effective content and messages.
  3. Aligned research and design efforts with business goals.
Personas & Process Maps

Understanding User Issues

My interviews with new users of this company's product indicated they did not understand their product. Business metrics indicated users called agents rather than completing tasks online. This research-based persona helped guide the design process for the life of the project.

Onboarding Process Map

By mapping out the company's onboarding flow, I prioritized areas where users could benefit from new educational content.

Reducing Confusion

Utilizing Business Metrics

By analyzing business metrics, specifically the top questions new customers asked during phone calls with agents, I selected the top three topics on which to educate users. The result was an onboarding experience that educated users about benefits of the product and encouraged them to enable app permissions.
