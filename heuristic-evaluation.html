Heuristic Evaluation For An Air Travel App

Summary

Project Directive

  1. Create a customer journey map for a customers:

    • Shopping for flights

    • Tracking flights of friends and family members

  2. Conduct a heuristic evaluation of the app's usability
  3. Outline design recommendations to improve UX

My Contributions

As the lead UX designer I on the project I:

  1. Completed all research and analyses.
  2. Crafted journey maps.
  3. Designed mockups with new UI recommendations

Heuristic Evaluation
Heuristic Observation
1 Efficiency of Use A flight search takes​ numerous variables into account. The​ “Recents” link allows users to quickly recall complex previous searches.
2 Visibility of System Status Segmented controls make it difficult to identify current selection. Does black or white mean it’s selected?
3 Understandable Interface Objects Icons in form fields quickly and accurately relay significance.​
4 Error Prevention Users should not be able to select invalid starting points and destinations.​
Heuristic Evaluation Continued
Heuristic Observation
1 User control and freedom Flight searches often have hundreds of results. Narrowing results to specific requirements expedites decision making.
2 Recover from Errors / Flexibility It’s easy for users to see if they chose the wrong date and identify which departure dates are cheapest.​
3 Discoverability What is the difference between Flex and Latitude? Any attempt to hide complexity will serve to increase it.​
